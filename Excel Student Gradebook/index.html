Excel Gradebook

A structured workbook used to record module tests, practical marks and continuous-assessment grades across the academic year.

Context

I used this Excel spreadsheet during my time as a teacher in Abu Dhabi. It allowed tracking of student attainment and, more importantly, simplified how I'd calculate the continuous-assessment portion of students' final grades.

Process

1

Plan Assessments

Mapped out the module tests, practicals and weightings for the academic year before building the workbook.

2

Build the Gradebook

Created the Excel structure with tables, columns and a layout ready to collect marks across the full year.

3

Future-Proof Formulas

Designed dynamic formulas so the weighting of each test and practical could be adjusted without rebuilding the sheet.

4

Track & Calculate

Entered marks throughout the year and used the gradebook to calculate each student's continuous-assessment final grade.

Reflections & Learnings

The continuous tracking of student attainment allowed for immediate constructive feedback to students and their parents, backed by data. It also allowed me to discuss grades and student progress very easily with my line manager. This was helpful as it gave my words more weight.

I refined the formula for calculating the portions each module test and practical contributed a few times until I settled on a dynamic one that allowed for flexibility.
